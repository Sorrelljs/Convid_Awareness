Convid-19 Self-assessment tool

Primary Notice:

COVID-19 should be taken seriously, but in many cases you can care for yourself at home, without visiting a medical facility.

This tool was developed using the latest recommendations from the CDC and vasts amount of research , which will help you determine what to do if you think you might have symptoms of COVID-19 or may have been exposed.

This Coronavirus Self-Triage Guide was developed to raise awareness and should not be used as a diagnosis. If you are feeling any symptoms mentioned above please visit your local doctor.
